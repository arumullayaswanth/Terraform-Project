Step 1: Configure Terraform Credentials in Jenkins

1. Open Jenkins Dashboard and go to **Manage Jenkins**.
2. Navigate to **Credentials** → **System** → **Global Credentials (unrestricted)**.
3. Click **Add Credentials** and configure the following:
   - **Kind**: Select **Secret Text**
   - **Secret**: Enter your **AWS Access Key**
   - **ID**: `accesskey`
   - **Description**: Enter a meaningful description
   - Click **Save**.
4. Add another credential:
   - **Kind**: Select **Secret Text**
   - **Secret**: Enter your **AWS Secret Key**
   - **ID**: `secretkey`
   - **Description**: Enter a meaningful description
   - Click **Save**.
